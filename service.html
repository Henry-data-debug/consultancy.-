Office Furniture

Fedama produces versatile wood desks with chrome or metal structures, along with customized meeting tables in stylish designs and sizes tailored to client spaces. Committed to promoting local products, the company has built strong, lasting relationships with its local clientele—key to its enduring success.

ICT Solutions

Fedama’s ICT division specializes in the supply, installation, and support of IT equipment—including hardware, software, cabling, and related services. We source cutting-edge technology tailored to client needs, delivering efficient solutions that empower businesses to focus on their core

Printing and Binding

Fedama offers hands-on printing and binding services, including spiral and soft (tape) binding, bulk printing, photocopying, and office stationery supply—delivering practical solutions with a personal touch that screens just can’t replicate

Building and constuction Materials

Fedama supplies a broad range of essential building and construction materials, including cement, timber, CGI iron sheets, nails, paint, binding wire, hinges, tower bolts, aggregates, blocks, and sledge hammers—ensuring quality and reliability for every project.

Imports and Exports

Fedama provides reliable import and export services, facilitating smooth cross-border trade of goods and materials tailored to client needs. With a strong logistics network and adherence to international standards, we ensure timely, secure, and cost-effective movement of products—supporting businesses in expanding their reach across markets with confidence

Graphic Design

Fedama provides creative graphic design and printing solutions alongside a wide range of branded gifts and promotional items. From flash disks, pens, key rings, mugs, and diaries to water bottles, we deliver customized merchandise that elevates brand visibility and leaves a lasting impression
